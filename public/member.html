    About This Project

    This is a collaborative project made by Team Savage Wood Skeletons. Food is something that people can always relate to, and we wanted to test a new technology. This gave way to the idea of making a mobile-first app that can scan for items you see in the store but may not know how to use. Hopefully you found this enjoyable.
